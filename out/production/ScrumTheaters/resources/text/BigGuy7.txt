BigGuy7
Robotics prodigy Guy lives in the city of Los Frantokyo. Next to his older brother, Modashi, Guy's closest companion is Maybax, a robot whose sole purpose is to take care of people. When a devastating turn of events throws Guy into the middle of a dangerous plot, he transforms Maybax and his other friends, Yo Yo Tomango, Sriracha, Lemon Pepper, and Derf into a band of high-tech heroes, in the hopes that the power of friendship can overthrow the danger.
